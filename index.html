Did you know?

World's Largest Democracy: India boasts the largest electorate globally? Over 900 million people are eligible to vote, making every election a thrilling spectacle.

Young Voters Take Charge: Over 19 crore (190 million) Indian voters are aged between 20 and 29. Understanding their aspirations is key to winning modern elections.

The NOTA Phenomenon: India's "None of the Above" (NOTA) option has been used by over 100 million voters since its introduction in 2013.

Election Commission Superheroes: India's Election Commission is the world's largest democracy watchdog, with over 3 million officials ensuring fair and free elections. That's more manpower than the entire Indian Army!
